Let's play Water, Fire and Plant!

Rules to the game

You will be playing against the computer.

You can choose between Water, Fire, and Plant.

The first one to three points wins.

Here are the rules to getting a point in the game:

  • Water beats Fire
  • Fire beats Plant
  • Plant beats Water

If the player and computer choose the same option (Ex. Fire and Fire), then no one gets the point.
